Privacy Policy

ReflexLab is a brain-training application. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can control your information.

Guest Mode

Play without creating an account.

Cloud Sync

Available after signing in.

Secure Login

Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address
  • Google name and profile picture (Google Sign-In)
  • Display name and optional profile image

Gameplay Data

  • Scores
  • Achievements
  • Daily Arena progress
  • Statistics and game progress

Advertising

Google AdMob may access your Advertising ID and related identifiers for ad delivery and analytics.

We do not collect your contacts, precise location, microphone, or browsing history.

How We Use Your Data

  • Save and synchronize game progress.
  • Authenticate and secure your account.
  • Provide cloud backup.
  • Display advertisements.
  • Schedule optional reminder notifications locally on your device.
